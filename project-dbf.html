Texas Design Build Fly Competition Team

RC Aircraft Project

Overview

This project documents my work on the University of Texas at Austin Design Build Fly (DBF) team, where we designed, fabricated, tested, and optimized a radio-controlled competition aircraft under strict performance, payload, and mission constraints. The project emphasized aerodynamic efficiency, structural integrity, manufacturability, and rapid iteration through testing.
